We hired humans, not robots.

Somewhere along the way, work stopped being about creativity and problem-solving, and became about moving data from Tab A to Tab B.

The "Copy-Paste" Purgatory

Your smartest people are spending hours manually reformatting spreadsheets and syncing data between tools that should already talk to each other. It's boring, and frankly, beneath them.

The Scavenger Hunt

"Is this the latest version?" "Where did you save that PDF?" "Did you check Slack?" The mental energy wasted just the work is often greater than the work itself.

Context Switching Burnout

Every time a notification pings or you switch apps, your brain loses focus. By 3 PM, your team is exhausted, not because they solved hard problems, but because they juggled too many small ones.

How We Work Together

A simple, structured process that keeps your team in the loop and turns vague “AI ideas” into concrete, low-risk projects.

01

Listen & Diagnose

We interview a few people on your team to understand how work really gets done today—no jargon, just plain language and screenshots.

02

Map the Work

Together we sketch your key workflows, highlight the “copy‑paste” moments, and agree on where automation will have the biggest impact.

03

Build & Pilot

We design lightweight automations or AI agents, plug them into your existing tools, and pilot with a small group before rolling out.

04

Embed & Improve

We train your team, document the new process, and tune the system based on real metrics like hours saved and error rates.
