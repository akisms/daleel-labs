The "Copy-Paste" Purgatory

Your smartest people are spending hours manually reformatting spreadsheets and syncing data between tools that should already talk to each other. It's boring, and frankly, beneath them.

The Scavenger Hunt

"Is this the latest version?" "Where did you save that PDF?" "Did you check Slack?" The mental energy wasted just the work is often greater than the work itself.

Context Switching Burnout

Every time a notification pings or you switch apps, your brain loses focus. By 3 PM, your team is exhausted, not because they solved hard problems, but because they juggled too many small ones.
