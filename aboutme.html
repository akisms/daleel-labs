About me

I’m building this consultancy because I keep seeing the same pattern: AI and automation are real, powerful, and confusing. A lot of teams want the benefits, but they don’t have time to chase every new tool or trend.

MY MISSION

Demystify, then deliver

I help small and medium businesses make sense of the AI + automation landscape, pick the right moves, and then actually build the workflows that save time, reduce errors, and help teams scale.

HOW I THINK

Calm systems beat flashy demos

I’m not here to sell “AI theater.” I’m here to build things that keep working on a random Tuesday when the team is busy.

Practical > perfect

What it feels like to work with me

I keep things simple and human. No jargon walls. No mystery meetings. You’ll always know what I’m building and why.

Plain language

I’ll explain options the way I’d explain them to a teammate. Clear tradeoffs, no fluff.

Low-risk rollout

We start with pilots, guardrails, and real metrics. You keep control.

Built to last

Documentation, training, and ownership matter. I don’t want you stuck needing me forever.
